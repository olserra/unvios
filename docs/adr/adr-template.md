# ADR Template

Use this template when creating new Architecture Decision Records in `docs/adr/`.

Filename: `YYYY-MM-DD-brief-title.md`

---

Date: <YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SSZ>

Status: Proposed / Accepted / Rejected

## Context

Explain the background and the problem being solved. Include relevant constraints and prior decisions.

## Decision

State the decision clearly. Include what code/config/files will be added or changed (paths and a short summary).

## Rationale

Why this decision was chosen. Trade-offs, alternatives considered, and why they were rejected.

## Migration / Rollback

Steps required to migrate to the new approach and how to rollback if needed.

## How to verify

Which checks, tests, or manual steps should be used to confirm the change works (CI, smoke tests, etc.).

## Next steps

Follow-ups required after acceptance (docs, TODOs, additional infra, etc.).

---

Keep ADRs short and actionable. Link to related PRs or issues when available.
